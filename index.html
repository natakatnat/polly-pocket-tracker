What's inside ✦
Everything Pocket Tracker can do for your collection
AI Set Lookup
Type any set name and instantly get year, maker, dolls, accessories, and collector pricing from Play Worn to Mint/MIB.
Photo Identification
Point your camera at any Polly Pocket compact and AI identifies the set name, year, and details — even from box art or listing photos.
Complete Piece Tracking
Log every doll and accessory you own. Hit − on anything not in your set and it moves to "Missing" — no strikethrough, just a clear red indicator.
Price Intelligence
Track estimated value and what you paid. One tap opens live eBay sold listings filtered to that exact set so you always know current market prices.
Wishlist with Priorities
Track grails and wishlist sets with Must Have / Want It / Nice to Have priority tiers. When you find one, "Got it!" AI-fills your collection form instantly.
Collection Sheet + Export
View all your data in a table. Export or share as formatted text, or download a JSON backup you can restore anytime — your data never leaves your device.
How it Works
From zero to a full catalogued collection in minutes
1
Search or snap a set
Type a set name in "Find any Polly Pocket set" or tap the camera icon to identify a set from a photo. AI returns the name, year, dolls, accessories, and prices.
2
Add it to your collection
Tap "+ Add to Collection" — the form opens pre-filled with everything AI found. Review and tweak anything, then move to Step 2 for the detail form.
3
Mark what's missing
In the dolls and accessories lists, tap − on anything you don't have. It moves to the "Missing from set" section so you know exactly what to hunt for.
4
Check what's included
Tap the chips for House/Compact, Box/Insert, Sticker Sheet, etc. to mark what came with your set. This drives the completeness % bar on your card.
5
Edit any time
Found a missing doll? Tap the ✏️ pencil icon in the top-right of any card to re-open the full edit form and update anything — dolls, accessories, value, condition.
6
Build your wishlist
Add sets you're hunting to the Wishlist tab. When you finally find one, tap "Got it! — AI fill details" and it automatically moves it to your collection with the form pre-filled.
